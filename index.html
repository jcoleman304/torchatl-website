About

A Sanctuary for
Serious Creators

You pull through the gate and the city disappears. The phone goes quiet. The only deadline is the one you set for yourself. By the second hour you've forgotten what building you're in — and that's the point.

TORCH is a private estate in Atlanta for creators who demand more than four walls and a console. No commercial building. No strangers. Just the room, the grounds, and whatever you came here to make.

Membership is capped at 24. Not because we can't grow — because this is a curated community of working professionals who value privacy, quality, and an environment that actually inspires.

Members don't book a studio. They come home to one.

Private Estate

A residential property, not a commercial building. Gated entry, private grounds, and complete discretion.

Curated Community

24 members maximum. Every person in the room earned their place. Quality over quantity, always.

Built for the Work

Recording rooms, guest suites, a full kitchen, and the kind of environment that stays out of your way.

TORCH membership is not for everyone. It's for serious creators who understand that environment shapes output.
