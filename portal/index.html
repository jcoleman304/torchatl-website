House Rules

Standards that protect the Torch experience

Torch ATL is a sanctuary for serious creators. These rules exist to protect the privacy, productivity, and experience of every member. Violations may result in membership review or termination.

Booking & Access

  • Book sessions in advance through the portal
  • No walk-ins or unscheduled visits
  • 30-minute grace period for late arrivals
  • 24-hour cancellation notice required
  • Operating hours: 10:00 AM - 2:00 AM

Guests

  • Pre-register all guests 24 hours before
  • Guest limits based on your tier
  • You are responsible for guest conduct
  • First-time guests must sign waiver
  • Guests must present valid ID

Privacy & Discretion

  • Never share the property address
  • No photography of other members
  • Never confirm other members' presence
  • No social media location tagging
  • Respect confidentiality at all times

Conduct

  • Maintain a calm, professional environment
  • Respect other members' sessions
  • Keep noise contained to your session
  • Clean up after your session
  • Report any issues to concierge

Prohibited

  • No smoking inside the facility
  • No illegal substances
  • No weapons on property
  • No unauthorized recording
  • No disruptive behavior

Consequences

  • 1st offense: Verbal warning (documented)
  • 2nd offense: Written warning
  • 3rd offense: Membership review
  • Severe violations: Immediate termination
  • No refunds for terminated memberships

By maintaining your membership, you agree to abide by these rules and understand the consequences of violations.
